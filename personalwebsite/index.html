Information on Islam

What is Islam?

Islam is a religion that began in Arabia in the seventh century and since then has spread to all around the world. Followers of the Islamic faith are called Muslims. The word Islam means "submission to God" and a Muslim is "One who submits to God." Muslims worship the the same God as Jews and Christians using the Arabic name Allah.
Muslims believe Islam to be the true religion that has existed since the beginning of time. They believe that a cycle of revelation, apostasy, and restoration occurred throughout history that ended with Muhammad and Gods revelation to him the Qur'an.
